Steps to Bake a Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (175C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Let the cake cool before serving.
