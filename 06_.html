Ashish Chanchanani

Chanchlani launched his YouTube channel, "Ashish Chanchlani Vines," in 2014. Initially sharing videos across various social media platforms, he eventually dedicated himself entirely to his channel. By the end of 2018, his channel had amassed 8.7 million subscribers. Chanchlani became one of India's fastest-growing YouTubers, reaching 10 million subscribers. His channel currently boasts 30.3 million subscribers and over four billion views.[4][5] Chanchlani's content often parodies Indian reality TV shows such as Sasta Big Boss and Sasta Shark Tank. His videos tackle relatable topics such as exams, office dynamics, and family life, embellished with humorous twists.[6] Chanchlani made his acting debut in 2016 in the television series "Pyaar Tune Kya Kiya." He was also featured in the ALT Balaji web series "Class of 2017," portraying Ishaan. Chanchlani has appeared in several music videos, including "Mera Baap Mahaan," "The Student Anthem," and "The Lockdown Rap."[7] He has collaborated with stars like Kartik Aaryan, Shahid Kapoor, and Akshay Kumar for advertisement shoots and promotions. He also made a cameo appearance in the Hollywood film "Men In Black: International," generating significant buzz.[8] Chanchlani has also directed the horror short film "Akhri Safar," which garnered close to 15 million views on YouTube. In 2021, he ventured into writing, directing, and producing with his show "ACV Classics."[9] Beyond content creation, Chanchlani engages in brand sponsorships and influencer marketing on Instagram, contributing to his status as one of India's prominent digital influencers, with a substantial Instagram following exceeding 16.7 million.[10] Additionally, he shares his insights as a speaker at industry events. In February 2024, he addressed the Great India Retail Summit in Mumbai, underscoring the rising influence of regional content creators in India.[11]

Chanchlani briefly managed his father's “Ashok Anil” multiplex. He had a passion for acting, which led him to enroll at the Barry John Acting Studio.
